Survival-Driven Gameplay

The Convoy BanovFrost is built for players who want the authentic struggle of DayZ winter survival without losing the heart of vanilla. Snowy valleys and frozen cities hide cartel stash houses, quest lines, hidden bunkers, and rotating custom loot events that reward exploration and risk.
